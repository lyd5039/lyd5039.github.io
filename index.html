About Me

I am a postdoctoral associate working with Prof. Francesco Monticone in the School of Electrical and Computer Engineering at Cornell University. Prior to this, I completed my Ph.D. in December 2023 under the supervision of Prof. Gennady Shvets in the School of Applied and Engineering Physics at Cornell University. I obtained my bachelor's degree from Case Western Reserve University (CWRU).

Research Interests: AI for science, complex networks, optical metamaterials and metasurfaces, quantum computing
